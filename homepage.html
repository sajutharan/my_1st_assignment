Quilling involves using strips of paper that are
rolled, looped, curled, twisted and otherwise
manipulated to create different shapes, and
glued together to create decorative designs.

The art of quilling works on fine motor skills including
coordination and strength. It is a great way to build
hand-eye coordination. Quilling is incredibly relaxing
and calming.
